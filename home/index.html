SOCIETY FOR COMMUNITY & COMMUNICATION DEVELOPMENT RESEARCH

OUR GOALS & OBJECTIVES

Conduct research and hold conferences/workshops focusing on in communication, education, health, agriculture, government and civil society relations or partnerships, NGOs and community relations, gender and youth issues in community development and the environment, that would promote sustainable development.
Development Catalyst
A catalyst for sustainable community development, providing relevant information
Development Solutions
Develop technologies (software and hardware) that will lead to community development
Facilitate Leadership
Facilitating community leadership in planning for science based solution to community development
Establish Networks
Establish a network of local and international experts that would continuously research into community and communication development

Who We Are |

We are an interdisciplinary body committed to the research and practice of communication and sustainable community development; non-religious, nonprofit, & open to academics, policy makers, and communication practitioners.

What We Believe |

That communities should be actively involved in how they want to develop and what they need for development. The people will need information as to what is available and possible for them to develop, and the sources of funds to finance their projects.

What We Do |

Conduct research in communication, education, health, agriculture, government and civil society relations or partnerships, NGOs, and community relations, gender and youth issues in community development and the environment.
